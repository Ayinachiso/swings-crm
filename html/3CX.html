3CX

Unleashing the power of seamless communication

Unified Communication Experience

By integrating 3CX with Swings CRM, you can create a unified experience that brings all communication channels into one central location. Whether it's voice calls, video conferencing, chat, or SMS, your team can access and manage all communication within the Swings CRM platform. This integration eliminates the need for multiple applications or switching between different tools and simplifies your workflow.

Click-to-Call and Pop-up Features

The 3CX integration enables click-to-call functionality directly from within the Swings CRM interface. This means you can initiate calls with just a single click, saving time and improving efficiency. Additionally, when a call is received, the integration triggers a pop-up display that shows the caller's information, including their contact details and history. This feature equips your team with valuable contextual information, allowing for a more personalized and informed conversation.

Streamlined Call Management

With 3CX integration, your team can manage calls seamlessly within Swings CRM. Incoming and outgoing calls can be logged automatically, eliminating the need for manual data entry. Call recordings can be stored and associated with relevant customer profiles, ensuring accurate documentation and facilitating future reference. This streamlined call management empowers your team to focus on customer interactions and provides a holistic view of your communication history.

Enhanced Collaboration and Mobility

Integration with 3CX enables real-time collaboration features such as video conferencing, screen sharing, and instant messaging. These tools allow your team to collaborate effectively, whether they are working remotely or in different locations. The mobility aspect of 3CX integration allows you to access and manage communication on-the-go through mobile devices, ensuring that your team remains connected and productive at all times.

Analytics and Reporting

By integrating 3CX with Swings CRM, you gain access to powerful analytics and reporting capabilities. This integration enables detailed call reporting, providing insights into call duration, response times, and customer interactions. These analytics facilitate data-driven decision-making, optimizing workflows, and enhancing customer service.
