INFORMATION ABOUT THE GENERAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATION (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area (EEA), we might gather and utilize your information. We will explain in this part of our privacy policy how and why we collect this data and how we keep it safe from being copied or misused.

WHAT IS GDPR?

GDPR is a law in the EU that ensures companies safeguard the data of EU residents and gives EU residents more control over their data.

WHAT IS PERSONAL DATA?

Any information related to an identifiable or acknowledged individual.

GDPR protects many types of information that could be used to identify a person, either alone or when combined with other information.

Personal data goes beyond just a person's name or email address. It includes things like financial details, political views, genetic information, biometric data, IP addresses, physical addresses, sexual orientation, and ethnicity. The Data Protection Principles requirements are as follows:

  • Personal data gathered should be handled fairly, legally, and clearly and only be utilized as an individual reasonably anticipates.
  • Personal data should only be gathered for a particular reason and solely utilized for that purpose. When organizations collect personal data, they must explain why they need it.
  • Personal data should not be stored longer than is required to serve its intended purpose.
  • People under GDPR can access their data and request a copy, update, delete, restrict, or transfer it to another organization.
WHY IS GDPR IMPORTANT?

GDPR introduces new rules on how companies should safeguard the personal data they gather and handle. It also raises the stakes for compliance by increasing enforcement and imposing more fines for breaches. In addition to these facts, it is just the correct action to take. At Help Scout, we believe strongly that your data privacy is more important, and we already have solid security and privacy practices in place, going beyond the requirements of this new regulation.
